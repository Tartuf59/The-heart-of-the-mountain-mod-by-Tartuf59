The Heart of the Mountain

Alpha Version 0.9.9

The Heart of the Mountain is a Minecraft mod by Tartuf59 that adds 2 ores, tools and armor. One ore is found in a secret laboratory, used to make 2 very rare and useful items for the player. This mod also adds 3 structures, 1 music disc, 1 book, and many challenges. The mod is translated into English and French.
