The heart of the mountain mod by Tartuf59

The heart of the mountain is a Minecraft mod that adds 2 ores, tools and armor for one, and for the other, which are found in a secret laboratory. This is used to make 2 very rare and useful items for the player. This mod therefore also adds 3 structures, 1 music disc, 1 book, and many challenges. The mod is translated into 2 languages, English and French.
