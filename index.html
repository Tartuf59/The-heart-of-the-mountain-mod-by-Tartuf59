The heart of the mountain

Alpha Version 0.9.9

The heart of the mountain is a Minecraft mod by Tartuf59 that adds 2 ores, tools and armor for one, and for the other, which are found in a secret laboratory. This is used to make 2 very rare and useful items for the player. This mod therefore also adds 3 structures, 1 music disc, 1 book, and many challenges. The mod is translated into 3 languages: English, French, and Breton.
